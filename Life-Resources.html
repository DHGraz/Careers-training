Mental Health in academia

Stress, depression and burn out are sadly common in academia. This section lists some resources:

  • Exploring Mental Health in Academia, article summarising recent studies and stats.
  • Postdocs under pressure: ‘Can I even do this any more?’ (2020), results of a survey on early career academics
  • Compilation of links by the University of Konstanz, from their PhD services network pages.
  • Mental Health in Academia: A question of support, part of a recent study on support for researchers (the final report has been published in November 2020).
  • The Dark side of academia, article describing three common challenges: rejections, imposter syndrome, and burnout.
At the University of Graz, there is a psychological support contact for students.
